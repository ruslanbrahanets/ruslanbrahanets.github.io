My services.

If you needed a web-site design, business card, logotype or you needed a high-grade turnkey web-site, landing, online-store you are in the right place! I can create all of this for you. Just give me the authority I need, and I will take care of the rest.

How does it works?

<1 — Analysis and data collection />

I communicate with you to understand the task. I'm testing hypotheses for interviews with users of your site

<2 — Prototyping />

I formulate an offer that solves the problem of users. I plan the logic of the page and place the blocks.

<3 — Visual design />

I create a graphic idea of the project, which stimulates the client to purchase. Combine the prototypes in the layout.

<4 — Layout ( if necessary ) />

I make up a site with a clean code. Then I improve the site with an attractive animation. If necessary, landing the site on the SMS or developing your own admin panel.
